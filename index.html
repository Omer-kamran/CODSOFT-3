SHAHID KHAN AFRIDI

A Player Way Ahead Of His Time!

Biography

Shahid Khan Afridi, also known as Boom Boom Afridi, is a former Pakistani cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the most iconic and flamboyant figures in the history of Pakistani cricket. Here is a brief biography of Shahid Afridi:

  • Full Name: Sahibzada Mohammad Shahid Khan Afridi
  • Date of Birth: March 1, 1980
  • Place of Birth: Khyber Agency, Pakistan.

    • Early Life:

  • Shahid Afridi was born in the tribal areas of Pakistan and hails from a Pashtun family.
  • He was educated in his hometown and initially played cricket in the streets before gaining recognition for his talent.

    • Cricket Career:

  • Shahid Afridi made his debut for the Pakistan national cricket team in October 1996 at the young age of 16.
  • He is primarily known for his aggressive batting style and powerful hitting. He holds the record for hitting the fastest century in One Day International (ODI) cricket, off just 37 balls.
  • Afridi was also a useful leg-spin bowler and took more than 500 wickets in international cricket.
  • He represented Pakistan in all three formats of the game: Test, ODI, and T20.
  • He was a vital part of Pakistan's 2009 ICC World Twenty20-winning team.
  • Afridi was known for his flamboyant personality and the nickname "Boom Boom" was given to him due to his explosive batting style.

    • Captaincy:

  • Afridi had multiple stints as the captain of the Pakistan cricket team, both in ODIs and T20Is.
  • Under his captaincy, Pakistan won the 2009 ICC World Twenty20 and reached the semi-finals of the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup.

    • Retirement:

  • Shahid Afridi announced his retirement from international cricket in February 2017.
  • He continued to play in domestic and T20 leagues around the world, representing various franchises.

    • Personal Life:

  • Shahid Afridi is a prominent public figure in Pakistan and enjoys a large fan following.
  • He is married and has five daughters.

Shahid Afridi's cricketing career and larger-than-life person have left an indelible mark on the sport and have made him a beloved figure in Pakistan and among cricket fans worldwide.

Records

  1. Fastest Century in ODIs (off 37 balls).
  2. Most Sixes in ODIs for Pakistan.
  3. Most Wickets in T20Is for Pakistan.
  4. Most Wickets in T20 World Cups for Pakistan.
  5. Only cricketer to score 1,000 runs and take 50 wickets in T20Is.
  6. Captain of Pakistan's 2009 ICC World Twenty20-winning team.
  7. Leading wicket-taker for Pakistan in ODIs.
  8. Long international career spanning over two decades.
  9. Equaled the record for the fastest century in T20 cricket (37 balls).
  10. Considered one of the most impactful all-rounders in limited-overs cricket.
